Question
1)	Show the details of the min salary holder of the employee table.
2)	Find the details of the most senior employee.
3)	Select all the employees who are earning same as BLAKE.
4)	Display all the employees who have joined after FORD
5)	List all the employees who are earning more than SMITH and less then KING.
6)	Find the employees who work in the same department with BLAKE
7)	Display all the salesmen who are not located at DALLAS.
8)	Select department name & location of all the employees working for CLARK.
9)	Select all the departmental information for all the managers.
10)	Display all the managers & clerks who work in Accounts and Marketing departments.
11)	Select all the employees who work in DALLAS.

Solve
1.select * from EMP where SAL=(select min(SAL) from EMP)
2.select * from EMP where HIREDATE=(SELECT min(HIREDATE) from EMP)
3.select * from EMP where SAL=(select SAL from EMP where ENAME='BLAKE')
4.select * from EMP where HIREDATE>(select HIREDATE from EMP where ENAME='FORD')
5.select * from EMP where SAL<(select SAL from emp where ENAME ='KING') and SAL>(select SAL from emp where ENAME ='SMITH')
6.select * from EMP where DEPTNO=(select DEPTNO from EMP where ENAME='BLAKE') 
7.select * from EMP where DEPTNO in (select DEPTNO from DEPT where JOB='SALESMAN' and LOC!='DALLAS')
8.select DNAME,LOC from DEPT where DEPTNO=(select DEPTNO from EMP where MGR=(select EMPNO from EMP where ENAME='CLARK'))
9.select * from DEPT where DEPTNO in (select DEPTNO from EMP where JOB='MANAGER') 
10.select * from EMP where JOB in ('MANAGER', 'CLERK') and DEPTNO in (select DEPTNO from DEPT where DNAME in ('ACCOUNTING', 'SALES'))
11.select * from EMP where DEPTNO=(select DEPTNO from DEPT where LOC='DALLAS')
