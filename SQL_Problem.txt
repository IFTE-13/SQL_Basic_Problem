Question:
1.	List all the emps except ‘PRESIDENT’ & ‘MANAGER” in asc order of Salaries. 
2.	List the emps whose Jobs are same as ALLEN.
3.	Display department-wise total salaries for all the Managers and Analysts, only if the average salaries for the same is greater than or equal to 3000.
4.	Display all the employees who are getting some commission in department 20 & 30
5.	Select details of all the employees working for FORD.
6.	Find out the job wise no. of Analysty and Clerk whose salary is more than 800.
7.	Find out the department where more than 3 emplyees are working.
8.	Show the unique department no from the em,p table.
9.	Increase the salary to 5% for the Analysts who joined in 1982. 
10.	Display job-wise maximum salary, minimum salary and average salary in a single query.
11.	Show the unique department no from the emp table.
12.	Increase the salary to 5% for the MANAGERS and SALESMSN who joined in 1981 
13.	Delete those employees who get commission as well as whose salary is greater than 1800.
14.	List the emps with departmental information whose BLAKE whose  Sal is more than ALLEN and job are same as FORD or BLAKE.

Solve:
1.select * from EMP where JOB = 'MANAGER' and to_char(HIREDATE,'MM-YYYY') = '03-1981'
2.select * from EMP where JOB = 'MANAGER' and MGR = (select EMPNO from EMP where ENAME='KING')
3.select * from EMP where ENAME like '%K_' and LENGTH(ENAME) = '5';
4.select * from EMP where ENAME like '__R%' and JOB in (select JOB from EMP where JOB like 'A%' or JOB like 'M%')
5.select * from EMP where JOB !='ANALYST' order by SAL DESC
6.select * from EMP where DEPTNO = (select DEPTNO from EMP where ENAME='SMITH') and ENAME != 'SMITH'
7.select DEPTNO,sum(SAL)from EMP where COALESCE(COMM, 0) !=0 and JOB in ('ANALYST','SALESMAN') group by DEPTNO
8.select * from EMP where MGR in (select EMPNO from EMP where JOB='MANAGER') and DEPTNO in (20,30)
9.select JOB,count(*) "PERSON" from EMP where JOB in ('ANALYST', 'CLERK') and SAL>800 group by JOB having count(*)>0
10.select JOb,count(*) from EMP group by JOB having count(*)>3
11.select distinct DEPTNO from EMP
12.select ENAME,SAL,(sal+(sal*0.05)) "NEW SALARY" from EMP where to_char(HIREDATE,'YYYY')='1981' and JOB in ('MANAGER','SALESMAN')
13.delete from EMP where COALESCE(COMM, 0) !=0 or SAL>1800
14.select * from DEPT where DEPTNO in (select DEPTNO from EMP where SAL > (select SAL from EMP where ENAME='ALLEN') and JOB in (select JOB from EMP where ENAME in ('FORD','BLAKE')))
