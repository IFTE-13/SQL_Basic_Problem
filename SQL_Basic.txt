Question

1.	List the Empno, Ename, Sal and 3 digit rounded Daily sal of all emps in the asc order of Annsal
2.	List the Empno, Ename, Sal of all emps working for Mgr 7369 
3.	Display all the details of the emps whose Comm. Is more than their Sal 
4.	List the emps who does not belong to Deptno 20 
5.	List the empno,ename,sal,deptno of the dept 10 emps in the ascending order of salary.
6.	List the emps whose annual sal ranging from 23000 to 40000
7.	List the emps working under the mgrs 7369,7890,7654,7900
8.	List the emps who are either ‘CLERK’ or ‘ANALYST’ in the Desc order of their salary.
9.	Find the analysts and managers (empno,ename,job,deptno) who’s salary is between 2000to 4000.
10.	Find the employees (empno,ename,hiredate,sal) who joned after 1982 and whose salary is greater than 1700 order by hiredate
11.	Find out the all salesman and analyst who belong from deptno 10 or 20 and whose salary is between 1500 to 2800.


1.select * from EMP order by EMPNO asc
2.select EMPNO, ENAME, SAL from EMP where MGR=7369
3.select * from EMP where COMM>SAL
4.select * from EMP where DEPTNO !=20
5.select * from EMP where DEPTNO = 10 order by SAL asc
6.select ENAME from EMP where sal*12 between 23000 and 40000
7.select * from EMP where MGR in (7369, 7890, 7654, 7900)
8.select * from EMP where job in ('CLERK', 'ANALYST')
9.select EMPNO, ENAME,JOB, DEPTNO from EMP where sal between 2000 and 4000
10.select EMPNO, ENAME, HIREDATE,SAL from EMP where HIREDATE >='01-JAN-1982' and SAL>1700 order by HIREDATE asc
11.select ENAME from EMP where JOB in ('SALESMAN', 'ANALYST') and DEPTNO in (10, 20)
