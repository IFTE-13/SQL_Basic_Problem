Solve the following Queries(Any 10):
1.	List the employees along with their sal+comm,
2.	List all the emps who belong from deotno 10 or 20 and who’s salary is more that 2500. 
3.	List the emps where third character of their name must be ‘A’ and last character is E.
4.	Get all the employees who work in the same departments as of SCOTT.
5.	Select all the employees who work in DALLAS.
6.	Select department name & location of all the employees working for CLARK.(Use mgr)
7.	List the name of the dept where more than 3 MANAGERS are working. 
8.	Display the second maximum salary.
9.	Update the department name from DALLAS to DHAKA.
10.	Find out the job wise average salary in the ascending order.
11.	Delete those employees from dept 10 or 20 who gets a commission. 


Solve
1.
select ENAME, sum(SAL+COALESCE(COMM, 0)) as "TOTAL" from EMP group by ENAME

2.
select * from EMP where DEPTNO in (10,20) and SAL>2500

3.
select * from EMP where ENAME like '__A%E';

4.
select * from EMP where DEPTNO = (select DEPTNO from EMP where ENAME='SCOTT')

5.
select * from EMP where DEPTNO = (select DEPTNO from DEPT where LOC='DALLAS')

6.
select DNAME,LOC from DEPT where DEPTNO in (select DEPTNO from EMP where MGR=(select EMPNO from EMP where ENAME='CLARK'))

7.
select DNAME from DEPT where DEPTNO like (select DEPTNO from EMP where JOB='MANAGER' group by DEPTNO having count(*)>3)

8.
select SAL from EMP where SAL in(select max(SAL) from EMP where SAL<(select max(SAL) from EMP))

9.
update DEPT set LOC = 'DHAKA' where LOC='DALLAS' 

10.
select JOB, round(avg(SAL),2) from EMP group by JOB order by JOB asc

11.
delete from EMP where DEPTNO in (10,20) and COALESCE(COMM, 0) !=0
